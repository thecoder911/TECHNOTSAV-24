About Us


The Department of Computer Science & Engineering, S. B. Jain Institute of Technology was established in 2008 and is accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with an "A" Grade and provides an outstanding Academic environment with excellence in teaching. Department conducts various Technical, co-curricular, and extra-curricular activities through various technical chapters and student forums throughout the year.
